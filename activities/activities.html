10 Activities To Try in Boracay

Pristine white-sand beaches, vibrant coral reefs, a thriving nightlife, and a plethora of fun-filled Boracay activities are just a few of the reasons why you should visit this tropical haven in the central Philippines! I've compiled the ultimate list of 10 Boracay Activities in this guide.

1. Parasailing

Parasailing is one of the coolest activities to do in Boracay. Parasailing Boracay will provide you with some of the greatest views of the entire island, particularly the shoreline. It is without a doubt one of the most thrilling activities on Boracay! During your Boracay vacation, you can choose to go on a single or tandem parasailing experience to see the beaches from a bird's-eye perspective.

2. Scuba Diving

One of the most popular Boracay sports is scuba diving. It is not only one of the most popular activities in Boracay, but also one of the top scuba diving locations in the Philippines. As a result, I recommend including a diving excursion on your Boracay visit. The rich undersea life will be seen straight off the nicest beaches in Boracay. You can go scuba diving even if you are a beginner because you will be guided by a PADI-certified divemaster who will teach you all you need to know.

3. Jet Skiing

You've probably observed that the majority of Boracay activities include water sports. Another item you should include on your Boracay schedule is a jet ski ride along one of the island's most popular beaches. A competent local English-speaking instructor will keep you safe on your jet ski while you enjoy an incredible trip above the turquoise water on Boracay's beaches. There are numerous jet ski adventure package options available, ranging from fifteen minutes to one hour.

4. Boracay Island Hopping Tour

A Boracay Island Tour will take you to some of the most gorgeous beaches in the area, as well as some of the most diverse aquatic critters. The Boracay Island Tour includes a cliff diving adventure at Magic Island, a visit to the popular Crystal Cove with local treasures on exhibit, and a great grilled buffet lunch on a gorgeous beach along the route.

5. Boracay Pub Crawl

A Boracay pub crawl will take you to five different exciting establishments. You will get the opportunity to visit BoracayÂ nightlife with 50 other people from all around the world and take advantage of exclusive beer specials and free shots at each club. A professional photographer will accompany you on the bar crawl to capture one of the most entertaining nights of your Boracay Island vacation.

6. Wakeboarding Experience

Wakeboarding is a popular Boracay pastime that involves standing up on a wakeboard in the water and being towed behind a motorboat. This Boracay water sports activity needs a lot of strength, but it is also certain to be a lot of fun. Wakeboarding sessions can range from 15 minutes to an hour in length.

7. Helicopter Tour

A Boracay helicopter trip will allow you to see the beauty of Boracay Island from above, with its breathtaking landscape, white sands, and blue water. Bring your camera to capture the beauty of the island from above as you go on one of the most thrilling Boracay activities!

8. River Tubing Experience

The River Tubing Experience at Malumpati Cold Spring is one of the most exciting things to do in Boracay. A one-hour trip from Boracay, you may float along the Philippines' cleanest inland bodies of water! This fantastic half-day excursion from Boracay includes convenient return transports from your hotel to the Malumpati Cold Spring. When you arrive, hop in an inflatable tube and drift downstream beneath a canopy of palm trees for the ultimate river tubing adventure!

9. Boracay Sunset Cruise

The sunsets on Boracay are among the best in the Philippines. A different approach to enjoy the sunset is to sail along the waters of Boracay Island on a boat. You'll get to visit the stunning beaches of Boracay while watching the sky become pink, orange, and scarlet throughout the 2-hour trip. While aboard the cruise, you will also be able to participate in water sports such as stand-up paddleboarding, snorkeling, and swimming.

10. Amazing Show in Boracay

If you're looking for something to do in Boracay at night, I highly recommend coming to the Amazing Show Boracay. There will be Latin dances, magnificent Dream Girls, and lavish shows by talented transvestites and transsexual entertainers. It is absolutely worth seeing the show, and it will be a spectacular night that you will only be able to experience on your Boracay vacation.
